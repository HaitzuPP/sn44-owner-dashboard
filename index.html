02 · Cost base (Part 1)
Monthly ops spend was the price of staying alive pre-dTAO
Sep 2024 – Jan 2025: 1,187 TAO (~$450K) sent to various addresses in regular monthly batches. Under the old Bittensor model, root network validators controlled which subnets received emissions. Subnet owners had to pay validators directly — or stake TAO to their hotkeys — to secure their vote. This was effectively a subscription fee. No payment, no emissions allocation.

03 · Cost base (Part 2)
The Feb 2025 spike was AMM seeding, not pure cost
On Feb 13 2025 — the day dTAO launched — the owner sent 1,963 TAO (~$785K) in a single batch to multiple addresses. This almost certainly seeded the initial SCORE alpha AMM pool with TAO liquidity. Without that seed, the alpha token launches with no price. This is capital deployment into an asset, not a sunk cost. They have since extracted ~$3.3M from that pool. ROI on the seed is the story.
