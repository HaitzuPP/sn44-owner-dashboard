02 · Cost base — pre-dTAO
Monthly validator payments were the price of staying alive
Sep 2024 – Jan 2025: 1,187 τ (~$450K) in regular monthly batches to root network validators. Under the old model, validators controlled which subnets received emissions — subnet owners had to pay them directly to secure their vote. This was a subscription fee. No payment, no emissions. Five months of outflows before a single alpha token was earned.
