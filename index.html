Largest sales happened well below the TAO peak
The two biggest liquidations — Aug 2025 (3,588 TAO, ~$1.28M) and Jan 2026 (4,639 TAO, ~$1.19M) — both executed when TAO was trading $257–$356. TAO peaked at $555 in Nov 2024, before dTAO launched.
